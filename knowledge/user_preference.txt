# University of Peshawar - Key Information
# This file serves as a placeholder for UoP-specific knowledge

## Quick Facts
- University Name: University of Peshawar (UoP)
- Location: Peshawar, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan
- Established: 1950
- Type: Public Research University
- Campus: Main Campus (Peshawar), Sub-Campuses (Abbottabad, Kohat)

## Key Officials (verify from homepage for current)
- Chancellor: Governor of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (ex-officio)
- Vice Chancellor: Chief administrative officer
- Registrar: Head of administrative matters
- Treasurer: Head of financial matters
- Controller of Examinations: Head of examination affairs

## Academic Structure
- Multiple Faculties including:
  - Faculty of Physical and Numerical Sciences
  - Faculty of Biological Sciences
  - Faculty of Humanities
  - Faculty of Social Sciences
  - Faculty of Arts
  - Faculty of Education
  - Faculty of Law
  - Faculty of Pharmacy
  - Faculty of Engineering and Technology

## Admission Categories
- A1: Open Merit (KP Domicile)
- A2: District Merit (KP Domicile)
- B: Non-KP Domicile
- C: Foreign/Overseas

## Programs Offered
- Undergraduate: BS (4 years), Associate Degree (2 years)
- Graduate: MS/MPhil (2 years)
- Postgraduate: PhD (3-5 years)

## Fee Structure (Approximate - verify from official sources)
- A1 Category (Subsidized): PKR 50,000-60,000 per semester
- B Category: PKR 100,000-150,000 per semester
- C Category: PKR 200,000+ per semester
